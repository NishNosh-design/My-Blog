Places Must Visit

Kota Kinabalu

i had visited in dec 2019

State capital of Sabah, Malaysia. It is also the capital of the Kota Kinabalu District as well as the West Coast Division of Sabah. The city is located on the northwest coast of Borneo facing the South China Sea.The Tunku Abdul Rahman National Park lies to its west and Mount Kinabalu, which gave the city its name, is located to its east. Kota Kinabalu has a population of 452,058 according to the 2010 census when the adjacent Penampang and Putatan districts are included, the metro area has a combined population of 628,725.

Auckland, New Zealand

i had visited in aug 2017

Auckland is New Zealand’s most populous city and is a multi-cultural hub of food, music, arts and culture. Also known as Tāmaki Makaurau, Auckland sprawls over volcanic hills and around twin harbours, offering an exhilarating mix of natural wonders and urban adventures.Auckland offers an urban environment where most people live within half an hour of beautiful beaches, hiking trails and a dozen enchanting holiday islands. Add a sunny climate, a background rhythm of Polynesian culture and a passion for outstanding food, wine and shopping, and you’re beginning to get the picture of Auckland, New Zealand, our largest and most diverse city.

Review On The Movies

Avengers Endgame
Nice and well directed film

I watched it in 2019 and it was amazing. It was a really nice and creative movie and very sentimental due to Tony Stark's death. I didn't know what to expect after Avengers: Infinity War but then I was really surprised at how good the movie was when I saw it. There were a lot of shocking moments and unexpected things. But, overall it was a fantastic movie. I gave it four stars instead of five because they could have killed another character instead of Ironman because he was my favourite character. :(

The Lion King
Nice and well animated film

It was a really well animated film because the graphics were very realistic and high definition.The lore was really well written but I could easily guess it. It will be prefect for kids below the age of five, so if you have any young siblings, you should show them this movie because this movie is perfect for that age category. The only reason I gave it 3 stars is because I already saw the cartoon version in 2015 which was 7 years ago so I already knew what was going to happen.
